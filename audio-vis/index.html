p5 Beats

p5 Beats is a collaborative interactive project by Paige McAllister and Christopher Lam. Users can create their own music by compiling beats amd loops. The music is visualized on screen and can even be downloaded and saved.

Inspired by programs like Garage Band and Windows Media Player's music visualizer, p5 Beats uses p5.js to not only connect audio and visual senses, but allow everyone to get creative and make some music themselves.
